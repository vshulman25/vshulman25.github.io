About Me

You can call me Vickee, I am a New Jersey native who has been living in Michigan since graduating from Michigan State in 2014. For the last 5 years, I worked in marketing representing major spirits brands throughout Detroit. I decided to make a career change this year and have started my next adventure in web development with the help of Michigan State's Full Stack Boot Camp. During the summer month, I try my hardest to get to the water - whether exploring the Great Lakes or enjoying the Atlantic ocean with my family. I am snack advocate, gadget enthusiast and eager to please on behalf of my clients.
